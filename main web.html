climate change

About This Website

Our mission is to educate, empower, and engage communities in understanding and addressing climate change, with a focus on Malawi.

Climate Change Explained

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns, primarily driven by greenhouse gas emissions from human activities.

Key Causes: Fossil fuels, deforestation, industrial processes, and agriculture.
